GAME CONCEPT

A 2D card deckbuilding roguelike with an educational hook. Players collect and combine animal cards modified by colors, adjectives, and verb action cards to battle mobs. Designed for all ages - kids learn real animal facts, colors, and vocabulary while adults get deep strategic gameplay.

Design North Star:
- Easy to read in one glance: animal = body, color = passive role, adjective = premium modifier, verb = action
- Cozy and collectible first, strategic second
- Every subsystem does a different job
- Runs create stories (a Bunny becomes a Blue draw engine, gains BIG and FAST, wins by cycling Scratch and Run Away)

Important Design Rules:
- No shirk (no false gods, idol worship, or polytheistic lore themes)
- Cards use Area2D NOT CharacterBody2D
- MobContainer is the ONLY thing swapped between encounters
- CanvasLayer uses top-left origin for all UI positioning

CARD FLOW RULES

- Animal cards: played to field slot, stay until defeated, defeated goes to graveyard, recycled into deck
- Color and Word cards: apply effect immediately, go to graveyard, recycled into deck, never stay on field
- End of turn: unused hand cards recycled to graveyard
- Draw 5 new cards each turn
- If deck reaches 0: shuffle graveyard into deck, draw remaining cards needed to fill hand to 5

---

THE FOUR CARD LAYERS

1. Animal Cards (the body)
- 12 animals across 3 types
- Speed: Rabbit / Deer / Cat / Frog / Fox - fast, draw/tempo, card velocity, evasion
- Defense: Turtle / Sheep / Cow / Chicken - high HP, healing, armor, stall
- Power: Lion / Bear / Dog / Crow - big hits, burst windows, attack multipliers
- Rock-paper-scissors: Power beats Defense, Defense beats Speed, Speed beats Power
- Small edges not hard counters (kid: flat nudge, advanced: percentage based)
- Animals have stage progression (example: Bunny -> Rabbit -> Fluffle)

2. Color Cards (passive role modifier)
- Blue: Draw +X
- Red: Rage / ATK boost
- Green: Heal +X
- Yellow: Energy / extra actions
- Purple: Shield
- White: Balanced stat boost
- Colors are PERMANENT for the run when applied
- Any animal can wear any color

3. Adjective Cards (premium modifier)
- BIG, FAST, TOUGH, SHARP, SMART, MAGIC
- Up to 3 adjectives can stack on one card
- Adjectives are rarer than colors

4. Verb Cards (action cards)
- Damage: Scratch, Bite, Pounce, Roar
- Defense: Hide, Block, Curl Up, Guard
- Draw/Speed: Run Away, Scout, Dash, Sneak
- Tempo: Hurry, Swap, Call Friend, Quick Move
- Magic/Trick: Trick, Confuse, Shield Spell, Copy

---

TWO BALANCE MODES (One Card Pool)

System         | Kid Version              | Advanced Version
Stats          | Small whole numbers      | Larger values plus growth curves
RPS            | Flat bonuses             | Percent-based modifiers
Colors         | Static stage scaling     | Stage scaling plus archetype hooks
Adjectives     | Large flat bonuses       | Multipliers, dodge, crit, block, engine text
Verbs          | One clear effect         | Conditional effects, scaling, sequencing payoff

---

TURN FLOW (Slay the Spire style)

1. Draw phase - draw 5 cards
2. Player turn - play cards using energy
3. Animals attack mob at end of turn
4. Mob attacks animals, player takes damage if field is empty
5. End turn - discard hand, draw new hand
- Energy resets to 3 at start of each turn
- Block/defense does NOT carry over between turns
- Hand cap: 10 cards maximum

RABBIT FAMILY DESIGN

Bunny (Stage 1 - Speed type)
- Kid: HP 12, ATK 4, DEF 2 | Passive: Draw 1 card when played
- Advanced: HP 18, ATK 6, DEF 3 | Passive: Draw 1. If 3+ cards in hand, +1 ATK this turn

Rabbit (Stage 2 - Speed type)
- Kid: HP 20, ATK 7, DEF 4 | Passive: Draw 1. +2 ATK if Verb played this turn
- Advanced: HP 30, ATK 11, DEF 6 | Passive: Draw 2. If last card was Verb, +15% ATK and draw again

Fluffle (Stage 3 - Merge: 3 Bunnies = 1 Fluffle)
- Kid: HP 35, ATK 12, DEF 7 | Passive: Draw 2. All Speed animals +3 ATK
- Advanced: HP 55, ATK 18, DEF 11 | Passive: Draw 3. Speed animals +20% ATK. If hand hits 5+, deal 5 to all enemies
- Merge cost: 0 energy (3 cards sacrificed is the cost)
- Fluffle stays permanently in deck for the rest of the run

Rabbit Color Variants:
- Blue = Snowshoe Hare (Draw 2 instead of 1)
- Red = Volcano Rabbit (endangered, Mexico - +4 ATK, loses draw)
- Green = European Hare (Heal 3 HP when drawn)
- Yellow = Jackrabbit (Play extra Verb this turn)
- Purple = Himalayan Rabbit (Gain 4 Shield when played)
- White = Angora Rabbit (+2 to all stats)

GAMEMANAGER SCRIPT (gamer_manager_node.gd)

Key functions built:
- build_starting_deck() - 10 Strikes + 4 Defends + 3 Bunnies
- start_turn() - resets energy, draws 5, guarantees 2 Bunnies on turn 1
- end_turn() - calls resolve_combat(), discards hand, draws new hand
- draw_cards(amount) - draws with hand cap and graveyard refill
- resolve_combat() - animals attack mob, mob attacks animals, player takes damage if field empty
- play_animal_card() - places on field, free, no energy cost
- play_attack_card() - deals damage to mob, costs energy
- play_skill_card() - gives player defense, costs energy
- play_color_or_word_card() - applies buff to field animals, costs energy
- win_combat() - mob HP reaches 0, game pauses
- lose_combat() - player HP reaches 0, game pauses
- take_mob_damage(amount) - reduces mob HP, checks win
- take_player_damage(amount) - reduces player HP after defense, checks loss
- spawn_hand_visuals() - spawns card scenes in hand zone
- spawn_field_visuals() - updates field slot display
- update_hud() - updates all labels on screen
